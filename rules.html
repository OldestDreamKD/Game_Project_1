Game Rules

1. Match Format

  • Each match is a 1 vs 1 face-off.
  • Players alternate between two roles each round:
    • Card Presenter: Selects a hidden card from a random concept category (e.g., Cities, Sports, Emotions).
    • Guesser: Asks strategic questions to identify the card’s concept.

2. Gameplay Rules

  • The Guesser may ask up to four (4) questions per round.
  • The Card Presenter can only respond with “Yes” or “No.”
  • After using or skipping questions, the Guesser must make one final guess.
  • If the guess is correct, the Guesser wins the round; otherwise, the Card Presenter earns the win.

3. Scoring System

  • For every unused question, the round winner receives +1 bonus point.
  • If both players finish with the same total score, a Tiebreaker Round is initiated.

4. Tiebreaker Rules

  • During a tiebreaker, both players draw from the same concept category again.
  • The number of allowed questions decreases by one each subsequent tiebreak (e.g., 4 → 3 → 2 → ...).
  • Tiebreakers continue until a clear winner emerges.

5. Tournament Progression

  • The winner of each match advances to the next round in a knockout format.
  • Play continues until one final champion remains.

6. Concept Selection Rules

  • A Card Presenter cannot select the same concept they used in their previous round.
  • They may select a concept that was previously chosen by their opponent.
  • Players are encouraged to use concept selection as part of their strategic gameplay.
