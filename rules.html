Game Rules

1. Match Format

  • Each match is 1 vs 1.
  • Players alternate roles each round as:
    • Drawer: Picks a card from a random concept set (e.g., Prime Numbers, File Types).
    • Questioner: Asks questions to guess the concept.

2. Gameplay Rules

  • The Questioner can ask up to 4 questions per round.
  • The Drawer may only respond with “Yes” or “No.”
  • After questions, the Questioner must make a final guess.
  • If the guess is correct, the Questioner wins; otherwise, the Drawer wins.

3. Scoring System

  • Each unused or unasked question adds +1 bonus point to the round winner.
  • If both players tie in total score, a Tiebreaker Round is triggered.

4. Tiebreaker Rules

  • In a tiebreaker, players draw again from the same concept set.
  • The number of allowed questions reduces by 1 each time (e.g., 4 → 3 → 2 → ...).
  • Continue until a winner emerges.

5. Progression

  • The winner advances to the next round (knockout style).
  • Continue until one final winner remains.

6. Concept Selection Rules

  • A Drawer cannot pick the same concept used in their previous round.
  • They may choose a concept their opponent used earlier.
  • Players may reuse or avoid previously seen concepts as part of their strategy.
